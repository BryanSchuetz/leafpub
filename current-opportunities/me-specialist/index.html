M&E Specialist

  • Project: Provision of Support for Improve Local Level Service Delivery and Economic Growth in Indonesia
  • Lot: DFAT
  • Assignment Duration: 3.5 years
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: DFAT/AOP/006
  • Location: Jakarta, Indonesia
  • Estimated Start Date: December 2014
  • Application Deadline: 1 September 2014

The overarching purpose of this work will be to support the Government of Indonesia (GoI) to strengthen the capacity of all levels of Indonesian government and communities for improved services and economic growth.

Three substantive pillars will support the long-term goal of a prosperous and stable Indonesia, by contributing to the development of active, inclusive communities and of a capable, responsive state as summarized below, interacting as necessary with sectoral investments under the frontline framework:

  • Work under Pillar 1 will foster active, inclusive communities to support community development in Indonesia;
  • If implemented under Pillar 2, AIPD2 (Australia-Indonesia Partnership for Decentralisation) would foster a capable, responsive Indonesian state by supporting central and subnational governments for improved service delivery; and
  • If implemented under Pillar 3, NGO support and strengthening would support efforts to foster active, inclusive communities and a capable, responsive Indonesian state.
