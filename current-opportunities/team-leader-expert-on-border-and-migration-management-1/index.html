Framework Contracts

Team leader/Expert on Border and Migration Management

  • Project: SUPPORT THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE VISA LIBERALISATION ACTION PLAN (VLAP)
  • Lot: BENEF 7
  • Assignment Duration: 15 working days (maximum of 5 days home-based)
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: EuropeAid/132633
  • Location: Chisinau and other sites related to implementation of VLAP
  • Estimated Start Date: 5 September 2014
  • Application Deadline: 18 August 2014

Global objective:

The global objective of this assignment is to assess the implementation performance of the Support to the Implementation of the VLAP.

Specific objective(s):

The specific objectives of this assignment are:

  • To evaluate the Government of Moldova compliance with the General Conditions defined in Section 3 of Appendix 2 and the relevant Specific Conditions defined in Section 4 of Appendix 2 of the Financing Agreement on Support to the Implementation of the VLAP, for the disbursement of the 1st instalment (see the Financing Agreement attached).
  • To advise the European Commission on payment (total, partial, or non-payment) of the 1st instalment of the Support to the Implementation of the VLAP.

Team leader/Expert on Border and Migration Management

Qualifications and skills

  • At least Master Degree in law or any filed related to border and migration management, or a university degree in the above areas with equivalent professional experience of at least 15 years in the relevant field;
  • Knowledge of the EU procurement rules and excellent reporting and drafting skills;
  • Fluent in written and spoken English. Knowledge of Romanian or Russian would be an asset;
  • Knowledge of the Moldovan context would be an asset.

General and specific professional experience:

  • A minimum of 12 years of professional experience in border or migration management;
  • A minimum of 5 years of professional experience in border or migration agencies, preferably in CIS or/and a country covered by the European Neighbourhood Policy;
  • Extensive knowledge of the latest developments and best practices in Integrated Border Management and migration policies;
  • Experience of working with various levels of Government;
  • Knowledge of EU external aid procedures, project cycle management principles and budget support methodology and evaluation;
  • Experience in performance assessment of EU budget support programmes;
  • Leadership skills to manage and motivate a team and to interact with project partners, the European Union Delegation to Moldova, civil society and the donor community;
  • Experience in project management and preparation.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com
