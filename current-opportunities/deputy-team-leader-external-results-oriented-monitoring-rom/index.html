Framework Contracts

Deputy Team Leader, External Results Oriented Monitoring (ROM)

  • Project: External Results Oriented Monitoring (ROM) reviews and support missions concerning projects and programmes financed by the European Union for the European Neighbourhood region
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: long-term (duration to be advised)
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: EuropeAid/134965/C/SER/Multi
  • Location: Brussels (full-time)
  • Estimated Start Date: December 2014
  • Application Deadline: 21st August 2014

Results Oriented Monitoring (ROM) reviews aim to assist the European Commission’s (EC) services and EU Delegations (EUD) in monitoring of and reporting on EU financed development and cooperation projects and programmes (hereafter “projects”) in third countries through independent reviews of the progress of their implementation and the achievement of project results. Compared to the ROM reviews conducted over the past decade, ROM reviews will proceed on a number of reforms that aim to improve the quality and the focus in coverage of the ROM reviews and take into account the high level of devolution reached over the past decade for project preparation and monitoring tasks to EUDs at field level.

This lot covers the EU funded national and regional projects and programmes whether single or multi-country and also Cross-Border-Cooperation (CBC) projects and programmes. These projects are implemented in the countries covered by the European Neighbourhood and Partnership Instrument (ENPI) and the European Neighbourhood Instrument (ENI) in Algeria, Egypt, Israel, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Syria, Tunisia and Palestine (Neighbourhood South); Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Georgia, Moldova and Ukraine (Neighbourhood East); Russia. If relevant, including partner countries other than ENI Southern Partner Countries (ENISPCs) :Bosnia and Herzegovina, Albania, Mauritania, Montenegro, Turkey, Bahrain, Comoros, Djibouti, Iraq, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Somalia, Sudan, United Arab Emirates and Yemen, when associated to the activities foreseen in regional programmes.

A Team Leader will head a team of key experts, comprised of the Deputy Team Leader and four other key experts who will organise and supervise the implementation of the contract, carry out ROM reviews, be in charge of the internal QC and will draft the annual as well as the end of contract consolidated ROM analysis. They shall cover, as much as possible, the following areas of expertise:

  • Education
  • Employment and social protection
  • Energy
  • Good Governance - Democracy, rule of law, human rights; Public administration, including decentralisation; PFM and macroeconomics stability (related to Budget Support)
  • Health
  • Natural resources, environment, climate change
  • Trade and Private sector development including: Regional integration; Business environment
  • Sustainable agriculture, food and nutrition security
  • Transport
  • Development and security nexus, including drugs and migration
  • Civil society
  • Statistics and performance measurement

We are looking for 2 Deputy Team Leaders - once covering ACP countries and the other covering ENPI countries. This position is a full time position (220 man days/ year).

The Deputy Team Leader (DTL) supports the Team Leader (TL) in fulfilling his/her tasks and responsibilities. He/she is in charge in particular of the application of the internal QC system by ROM QC experts and all ROM experts. He/she drafts the quarterly QC reports and follows-up the recommendations of the QA reports. Like the TL, he/she implements ROM reviews as a sector expert for projects and programmes and plays the role of a ROM QC expert.

Qualifications and skills

  • He/she has a university master degree (or equivalent).
  • He/she must be fluent both in French and English.

General Professional Experience

He/she has a minimum of 15 years of experience in development cooperation, notably in project management with a minimum of 5 years of experience as Deputy TL or TL preferably of a mixed team of long and short term experts.

Specific Professional Experience

  • He/she has a minimum of 10 years of experience in one of the abovementioned areas of expertise. Experience in other of the above mentioned areas of expertise is an asset.
  • He/she has a minimum of 5 years of experience in results based approaches, monitoring, and/or evaluation.
  • He/she has a minimum of 3 years and preferably 5 years of experience in quality systems in services sectors: improving work processes and efficiency, defining quality policies and objectives, implementing quality procedures.
  • He/she or the TL has a minimum of 5 years of experience on Neighbourhood countries.
  • He/she has preferably a good knowledge of EU and other donors implementing modalities.
  • He/she has preferably experience in gender mainstreaming related to his/her area of expertise.

If you meet the criteria and would like to be considered for this nominated role please email your CV in EC format with a covering note outlining how you fit the requirements. Please email your application to sarah_owen@dai.com quoting (ROM/DTL) in the subject line of your email.

Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

Please note that this is a long-term EU-funded project not a EC COM Lot 1 Framework project.
