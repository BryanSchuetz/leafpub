Senior Expert in PSD/Finance/Trade with expertise in Adult Training and EU Policies

  • Project: Effective Design of Trade and Private Sector Development Assistance
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: upto 13 months
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/347499 - Version 1
  • Location: All Countries
  • Estimated Start Date: 30 September 2014
  • Application Deadline: 21 August 2014

The specific objective of the assignment is to assist DEVCO.C4 in the organisation, preparation and delivery of the training programme on Trade and Private Sector Development (TPSD) for EU staff, both at Headquarters and in Delegations in third countries.

In particular, the expert will assist DEVCO.C4 with:

  • the design and delivery of the TPSD training, presenting different modules, to be agreed before each training session,
  • the maintenance and updating of training material, including e.g. the regular updating of the electronic training repository (containing the different presentations, technical notes, handouts and exercises), the creation of new training modules,
  • the logistical organisation of the training sessions in Delegations, including, among others, the preparation of course handouts (printed material, USB sticks), assisting trainees in the preparation of case studies to be presented during the training, the reservation of training rooms and equipment when abroad
  • the course evaluation with a view to improving the overall quality training and
  • the administrative management of hiring additional experts with specific expertise.

Senior Expert in PSD/Finance/Trade with expertise in Adult Training and EU Policies

The Expert will comply with the following requirements, as regards to education, skills and type of experience:

  • Master’s degree in a field related to one or more of the areas to be addressed during the training, or equivalent experience;
  • At least 10 years' documented work experience in the identification, design and implementation of PSD, access to finance and Trade Related Assistance programmes in developing countries ;
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills;
  • Excellent facilitation skills, with a focus on hands-on practical training;
  • Extensive experience in design and delivery of adult training;
  • Proficiency in English, and a working knowledge of French and/or Spanish is a plus;
  • Good reporting skills;
  • Well-informed about EU trade policy and EU external aid policies;
  • Conversant with the principles and methodologies of project cycle management and aid delivery.

Assignment location: Training in Brussels and in regional location (Mozambique indicatively envisaged at this stage, TBC)

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to Francesca_Giordano@dai.com
